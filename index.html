About Latirigi Solutions Ltd

Latirigi Solutions Ltd is Kenya’s leading recruitment agency, dedicated to bridging the talent gap between employers in the Gulf and Middle East and Kenya’s pool of skilled professionals. Fully accredited and licensed by the Ministry of Labour under the National Employment Authority (NEA), Latirigi Solutions specializes in overseas manpower export, providing transparent, efficient, and quality-driven recruitment services.

Our team is committed to aligning talent with opportunity, enabling Kenyan professionals to expand their career horizons abroad, while supporting businesses in the UAE, Qatar, Saudi Arabia, Bahrain, Oman, and other Middle Eastern countries in building productive, capable teams.

Our Mission

To deliver recruitment excellence and manpower solutions that empower Kenyan professionals to thrive in the Gulf and Middle East. Latirigi Solutions Ltd is dedicated to ethical, transparent practices, creating long-term value for our clients and candidates alike. Our mission is to ensure that each recruitment placement enriches both the candidate’s professional journey and the employer’s operational success.
